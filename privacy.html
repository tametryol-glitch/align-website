Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Align ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Align mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Account data — email address, display name, and profile photo when you create an account.
  • Birth data — date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth, which you enter voluntarily to generate astrological charts.
  • Social content — posts, comments, messages, photos, and other content you create within the app.
  • Preferences — notification settings, privacy settings, and app preferences.

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information — device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage data — features used, screens viewed, and interaction patterns (collected anonymously for analytics).
  • Push token — your device's push notification token, used solely to deliver notifications you have opted into.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized astrological charts, readings, and compatibility analyses.
  • To provide social features including messaging, friend connections, and community posts.
  • To send push notifications you have opted into (transit alerts, moon phases, messages).
  • To improve app performance, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • To process subscriptions and in-app purchases via third-party payment processors.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only in these circumstances:

  • With other users — your public profile, posts, and content you choose to share are visible to other users based on your privacy settings.
  • Service providers — we use Supabase (database and authentication), Railway (API hosting), RevenueCat (subscription management), and Expo (push notifications). These providers process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements.
  • AI services — chart interpretations may be generated using OpenAI's API. Birth data sent to the AI is not used to train models and is not stored by the AI provider.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (hosted on AWS) with row-level security policies that ensure users can only access their own data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption. We implement industry-standard security measures, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Export — you can view all your data within the app and request a data export from Settings.
  • Delete — you can delete your account and all associated data from the app settings. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Privacy controls — you can control who sees your birth data, online status, and friend list via in-app privacy settings.
  • Notifications — you can manage or disable push notifications at any time from the app settings or your device settings.
  • Withdraw consent — you may stop using the app at any time. For EU/EEA users, you may withdraw consent for data processing by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

Align is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

7. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
