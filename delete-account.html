Delete Your Account

Last updated: April 15, 2026

You can permanently delete your Align account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how, what gets removed, and what (if anything) is retained.

Warning — this action is permanent.

Once your account is deleted, your birth data, charts, readings, messages, posts, and saved content cannot be recovered. If you have an active subscription, cancel it first through the App Store or Google Play so you are not charged again.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the Align app on your phone.
  2. Go to the Profile tab in the bottom navigation.
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon in the top right).
  4. Scroll to Account and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your decision when prompted.

Your account and associated data will be deleted immediately from our active systems. Encrypted backups are purged on the schedule described below.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to Ortsallc@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line "Delete My Align Account".
  3. Include the email address you used to sign up so we can locate your account.

We will confirm receipt within 2 business days and complete deletion within 30 days, in line with GDPR Article 17 and the Google Play User Data policy.

What data is deleted

  • Account identity — email, display name, profile photo, authentication records.
  • Birth data — date, time, and place of birth; saved charts and reports.
  • Readings and interpretations — natal, transits, synastry, returns, tarot, numerology, and AI conversation history.
  • Social content — posts, comments, reactions, messages, voice messages, friends, follows, and bookmarks.
  • Preferences — notification settings, privacy settings, push tokens, device identifiers.
  • Subscription metadata stored in our database (your store receipts remain with Apple or Google).

What we may retain

A small amount of information may be retained where we have a legal obligation or legitimate interest:

DataReasonRetention
Transaction / subscription recordsTax and accounting obligationsUp to 7 years
Abuse / safety recordsEnforce Community Guidelines, prevent ban evasionUp to 2 years
Encrypted backupsDisaster recoveryPurged within 30 days
Anonymous usage analyticsProduct improvement (not linkable back to you)Indefinite, anonymized

Cancel your subscription separately

Deleting your Align account does not automatically cancel an active subscription, because purchases are managed by the app stores, not by Align. Cancel in advance:

  • iOS: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Align → Cancel Subscription.
  • Android: Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Align → Cancel.
